Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Dodgy Artists! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information, including:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you provide to us when you sign up for our newsletter, contact us, or make a purchase.
  • Usage Data: This includes information about how you access and use our website, such as IP addresses, browser types, and device information.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Respond to your inquiries or requests
  • Process transactions or orders (e.g., merchandise, sound packs, beats)
  • Send you promotional materials, newsletters, or updates with your consent
  • Analyze website usage to improve user experience

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information you provide. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party providers who help us operate our website or provide services, such as payment processing and shipping.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or if we believe such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Request that we stop processing your personal information in certain situations.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact details below.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update this page. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or changes.
